About Us

"Myself Govardhan Darade I reside on a hilltop in the small village of Khadki in Beed District. Coming from a poor family, my parents used to work as Sugarcane cutting workers. Despite all this, I managed to pass my 10th and at age of 16 I started working with a sickle. I continued cutting off Sugarcane for 2 long years, which was a family tradition. Like struggling for 2 long years, I shifted from my Sugarcane cutting worker to vehicle cleaner and then to the Mechanics side in charge of a company. Struggling for years and learning many things, I still used to feel conscious. I always used to think that the kind of environment I grew up I didn't want anyone to face that kind of situation anymore. And then I thought of taking the task of shaping their life into my hands. Around that time I left my well-paying job and started NGO Pasaydan Seva Prakalp. Our NGO has already shaped various life and will do the same in the future. And this would be successful by kind helping hands".
